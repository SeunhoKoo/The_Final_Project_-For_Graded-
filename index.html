Korea's most famous beach with golden sand, crystal clear waters, and vibrant nightlife.

Why Visit Busan?

Beautiful Beaches

From the famous Haeundae to the scenic Gwangalli, Busan offers some of Korea's most stunning coastal experiences.

Rich Culture

Experience vibrant street art, traditional temples, and the internationally acclaimed Busan Film Festival.

Amazing Food

Indulge in fresh seafood, authentic Korean cuisine, and unique local specialties that you can't find anywhere else.

Diverse Landscapes

From coastal cliffs and sandy beaches to colorful hillside villages and urban skylines - Busan has it all.
